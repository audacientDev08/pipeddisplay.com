Turn your Android tablet
into a second display for your Mac.

Piped Display streams your Mac's screen to any Android tablet over USB, Wi-Fi, or an end-to-end encrypted Wi-Fi channel that can't be intercepted.

Features

Three ways to connect.
Zero compromises.

Use whatever works best for where you are — at your desk, on the couch, or somewhere you don't trust the Wi-Fi.

USB

Plug in and go. Lowest latency, highest reliability, perfect for serious work or anywhere battery matters.

Wi-Fi

Go wireless on your home or office network. Auto-discovers your Mac and connects in seconds — no cables, no fuss.

Secured Wi-Fi

Our encrypted channel keeps your screen private even on public Wi-Fi. End-to-end encryption — non-hackable, by design.

How it works

From download to dual-screen in three steps.

1

Install on Mac

Download the Mac app from this site and drag it to Applications. Grant screen-recording permission when prompted.

2

Install on Android

Grab the companion app from Google Play on your Android tablet — phones work too, but tablets are the sweet spot.

3

Pick your channel

Connect over USB, Wi-Fi, or the secured channel. Your tablet becomes a second display instantly.

Download

Get Piped Display.

Both apps are required. Install on your Mac and your Android tablet to start streaming.
